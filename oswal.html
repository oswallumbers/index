About Oswal Lumbers

With decades of experience in the timber industry, we are committed to delivering sustainable and high-quality wood products.

Our Legacy in Timber Excellence

Oswal Lumbers Pvt Ltd has been a trusted name in the timber industry since 1985. What started as a small sawmill has grown into one of the leading wood product manufacturers in the region.

We specialize in sourcing, processing, and supplying premium quality timber and engineered wood products to clients across various industries including construction, furniture making, and interior design.

Our commitment to sustainable forestry practices and quality control has earned us a reputation for reliability and excellence in the market.
